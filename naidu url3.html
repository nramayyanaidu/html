Learn English in Canada

English proficiency can open doors to so many opportunities. Learning and developing strong English
language skills is the key to reaching your educational, career, and life goals. With prestigious universities
and colleges; safe and clean cities and friendly, welcoming communities, it is no surprise that Canada is a
top destination for international students.


Study at Toronto, Ontario



ELS/Toronto is situated in Canada’s largest city, in the midst of the country’s commercial and cultural
center. This dynamic city Center is within walking distance of many attractions, including museums and
galleries, sports venues, and theaters. Venture outside of downtown to see the natural beauty of the
Toronto Islands. Trips to Niagara Falls, and tours of Ottawa, Montreal, Quebec, New York City, Chicago, and
Boston are among the most popular weekend activities.


Study at Vancouver, British Columbia



ELS/Vancouver is conveniently located in the heart of downtown Vancouver, a city of rare natural beauty
and diverse cultures considered to be the jewel of Canada’s West Coast. Vancouver is consistently ranked
among the most livable cities in the world, and enjoys comfortable temperatures all year round. Visits to
Whistler, Victoria and Seattle as well as multiday tours of the Rocky Mountains are popular weekend
student activities.

ELS English Courses in Canada

ELS offers a variety of English courses in Canada to help you gain the proficiency you need to be
successful.

Levels

ELS offers 12 proficiency levels (Beginner to Masters), as well as placement testing to determine the level that best
fits your abilities.

English for Academic Purposes

English for Academic Purposes (EAP)

The English for Academic Purposes Program is designed for international students who want to attend
one of the colleges or universities in Canada, including students who wish to apply for conditional
admission to an ELS partner institution.

 

This intensive ELS English for Academic Purposes (EAP) program can help prepare you for success in an
academic setting. Morning classes are dedicated to Structure and Speaking Practice or Language
Studies, Vocabulary Enrichment (in levels 101-109) or Skills Enhancement Classes (in Masters levels), and
a lesson in the Language Technology Center. Afternoon classes are dedicated to Reading and Writing.

General English

General English

Our General English program helps you communicate more accurately and efficiently. The program
supports you as you develop skills that will help you communicate in English-speaking situations.


Mornings will be spent studying the fundamentals of the English language through Structure and
Speaking Practice or Language Studies, Vocabulary Enrichment (or Skills Enhancement, in Masters
levels), and a lesson in the Language Technology Center. Your afternoons will focus on Skills
Enhancement Classes.

IELTS Preparation - Canada

IELTS Preparation - Canada

Take the IELTS™ exam with confidence in your test-taking and English abilities. The ELS IELTS Preparation
program will equip you with the English and test-taking skills needed to be successful on the IELTS exam.
Your morning classes will be devoted to general English skills, and you will prepare for the exam itself in
your afternoon classes. This course is designed for students in Levels 106 and above.

This course is consistently available in Vancouver; it is available in Toronto only as an elective when there
is sufficient demand.

 

  • ELS Levels Covered: 7 Levels: 106 (High intermediate) to 112 (Masters)
  • Number of ELS Locations Offered: 2 (Vancouver, BC and Toronto, BC (upon demand)
  • Lessons / Week: 30 (50 minutes each Monday – Friday)

TOEFL iBT Preparation - Canada

TOEFL iBT Preparation - Canada

The ELS TOEFL iBT® course prepares you for the exam and provides you with a variety of tools such as
TOEFL practice exams, textbooks, and advanced listening software. This course is designed for students
in Levels 106 and above.

 

This course is consistently available in Vancouver; it is available in Toronto only as an elective when there
is sufficient demand.

 

  • ELS Levels Covered: 7 Levels: 106 (High intermediate) to 112 (Masters)
  • Number of ELS Locations Offered: 2 (Vancouver, BC and Toronto, BC (upon demand)
  • Lessons / Week: 30 (50 minutes each Monday – Friday)
