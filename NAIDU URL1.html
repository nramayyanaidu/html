Learn English in India

India is a welcoming and educational atmosphere for students who are interested in improving their English
skills. When you choose India as your study destination, you will enjoy the same high-quality English programs
as you would experience in other parts of the world, combined with India’s affordable cost of living. In addition,
India’s visa and enrollment policies are straightforward and welcoming to international students from all over,
the world.

Choose Chennai


Studying in India offers you a strong, high-quality educational system with English-medium universities, high-achieving alumni, and proven leadership in STEM fields. In addition, studying in India will allow you to enjoy the country’s vibrant culture and rich history. Spend your free time sightseeing, enjoying local cuisine and cultural activities, and living comfortably within your means due to India’s affordable cost of living. Become a part of India’s proud 50-year history of supporting and enriching the lives of international students from all over the world!

Study at SRM University



SRM University, home to ELS/Chennai, is one of the highest-ranked universities in India, and offers a variety of
undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral degree programs. Students are drawn to the diverse and
welcoming campus of SRM University for its reputable programs, state-of-the-art facilities, cutting-edge
research, diverse faculty, and invaluable global networking opportunities. Scholarships are available for
international undergraduate students, adding even more value to the already-affordable tuition costs.

SRM students travel around campus either on foot or by bicycle. The university operates buses to transport
students into Chennai city and to other key points, and there are also public bus and rail stops not far
from campus. SRM is 40 kilometers from the center of Chennai and 20 kilometers from Chennai Airport,
making the campus easily accessible by road, rail, and air.

English for Academic Purposes (EAP)

The English for Academic Purposes program will not only help you prove your English proficiency – a requirement
for universities and colleges in pathway destinations such as the United States and Canada – but will also equip
you with the skills you need to be successful in any English-speaking academic setting, including at SRM University.
Learning English before you attend university will increase your confidence and allow you to focus on your studies,
rather than struggling to understand and be understood in your classes.

Morning classes are dedicated to Structure and Speaking Practice or Language Studies, Vocabulary Enrichment
(in levels 101-109) or Skills Enhancement Classes (in Masters levels), and a lesson in the Language Technology
Center. Afternoon classes are dedicated to Reading and Writing.

Key Facts
  • Age Requirement: 17+
  • Maximum Students/Class: 20 students (Skills Enhancement Classes); 15 students (all other classes)
  • Session/Level: 1 session (4 weeks) for each level

General English

Learn to communicate accurately and efficiently in English-speaking situations in the ELS General English
program. Speak confidently, improve your vocabulary, grammar, and listening skills, and network with other
English-speakers in a supportive and structured classroom environment.

In the mornings, you will focus on the fundamentals of the English language through Structure and Speaking
Practice or Language Studies, Vocabulary Enrichment (or Skills Enhancement, in Masters levels), and a lesson in
the Language Technology Center. You will focus on Skills Enhancement Classes in the afternoons.

Key Facts
  • Age Requirement: 18 (Center Director may approve students younger than 18 in certain instances)
  • Maximum Students/Class: 20 students (Skills Enhancement Classes); 15 students (all other classes)
  • Session/Level: 1 session (4 weeks) for each level

Semi-Intensive English

Truly experience your surroundings and immerse yourself in local culture while improving your English with the
Semi-Intensive English program. After completing your English lessons in the mornings, you will have the freedom
in the afternoons to explore the local area and practice the English skills you are learning.

Your mornings will be spent in the English classroom, focusing on the building blocks of English proficiency:
Structure and Speaking Practice or Language Studies, Vocabulary Enrichment, and a lesson in the Language
Technology Center.

Key Facts
  • Age Requirement: 18 (Center Director may approve students younger than 18 in certain instances)
  • Maximum Students/Class: 20 students (Skills Enhancement Classes); 15 students (all other classes)
  • Session/Level: 1 session (4 weeks) for each level

Yoga and Cultural Immersion

Study English while learning about Indian culture and practicing yoga – including subjects like practice of Asana,
Pranayama and meditation, yoga philosophy, and more. Enjoy the serene and peaceful surroundings of ELS/India

Morning classes consist of fundamental English language skills, with classes such as: Structure and Speaking
Practice, Reading and Writing, and Vocabulary Enrichment.
